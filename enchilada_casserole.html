Enchilada Casserole

This enchilada casserole is a delicious and easy-to-make dish that combines layers of tortillas, seasoned meat, cheese, and enchilada sauce. Perfect for a family dinner or a potluck, this casserole is sure to be a hit!

Ingredients

  • 9 corn tortillas
  • 1 pound ground beef or chicken
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 can (15 oz) enchilada sauce
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh, frozen, or canned)
  • 2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or a blend)
