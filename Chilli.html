Chili

Welcome to the best and simplest chilli recipe! This recipe is courtesy of my mother and it always was a fantastic meal during the winter.



Ingredients

  • 1 pound of 80/20 ground beef
  • 1 can of pinto beans
  • 1 can of kidney beans
  • 1 can petite tomatoes
  • 1 package of McCormick chilli mix
